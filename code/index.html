    why go vegan section

    There are so many benefits to a vegan diet. We know it can seem daunting if you’ve spent your life relying on animal-derived products. We understand the questions that might be running through your head. Will I have to eat bland food forever? Will I get all the nutrients I need? Will it cost me lots of money? Why go vegan?! Well, lucky for you, we’re here to help.

    Help Stop Animal Cruelty

    We wholeheartedly believe that this must change. Let’s look at some of the stats. Another of the main reasons to go vegan is to show care and respect for the wonderful creatures we live alongside. We all know that a diet laden with animal products means the violent killing of animals. And despite all the amazing vegan alternatives now available, this continues to be the norm.

    Eating Green for Protein

    Vegetables are often associated with vitamins, minerals, and fiber, but they can also provide a good source of protein, especially for those following plant-based diets. While vegetables may not be as protein-dense as meat or legumes, they still contain notable amounts of protein. For instance, vegetables like spinach, broccoli, peas, and Brussels sprouts are rich in protein and can contribute significantly to daily intake.
